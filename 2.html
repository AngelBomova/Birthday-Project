My 10th Birthday!

This birthday marked a milestone in my life finally being double digits! I do actually remember a lot about this day. I woke up and had Teras Bagel for breakfast and my friend came over and he went to Getair and jumped around for around 2 hours. We came home and then had cake. They went home and then later my parents took my brother and I to the Rainforest Cafe! This is a birthday tradition of mine ever since my 4th birthday. We had the volcano cake for dessert! This was one of my favorite birthdays because of the day itself and 2016 was a wonderful year.

My 18th Birthday!

This day has not yet happened yet but I already know it is going to be a good one, if not the best. To start I will finally be an adult which is a huge milestone and is probably the biggest birthday since the day I was born. On top of this I am going to Mexico the week before my birthday which will make it even better. I think I am going to invite some friends to dinner and then have some cake at my house. When talking to my friends who turned 18 they always talk about how they think about their childhood the whole day and I feel like this will happen to me. Being an adult is surreal to me and I am excited but also know it is a big step up from being a kid. I have to focus on college and my future with my job and life in general.
